About

Flight Space provides professional real estate photography in the central valley. We specialize in real estate photography for residential and commercial properties. Flight Space was launched in 2021 by Jeremy Cordero and is located in Fresno, CA. Flight space is dedicated to treating every client with respect and making their vision for the shoot come to life. We will be providing high quality photos and videos in a timely manner. We offer affordable upfront pricing so that you are not having to inquire about our prices. Showcase your listings from a new perspective and allow viewers to be sold before even speaking with you. Let the photography do the talking.
